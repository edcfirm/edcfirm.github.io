Project Overview

EDC Firm was engaged by CCC Engineering Ltd. (CCCE) to provide electrical design and consultancy services for multiple substations under DESCO as part of a turnkey EPC project.

Project Details

  • Client: CCC Engineering Ltd. (CCCE)
  • End Client: DESCO
  • Project Type: Substation Design & Consultancy
  • Project Value:
  • Status: 90% Completed

Substations Covered

  • 4 × 132/33/11 kV Grid Substations
  • 4 × 33/11 kV Distribution Substations

Scope of Work

Earthing / Grounding System
  • Soil resistivity analysis
  • Ground grid design
  • Step & touch voltage calculation
Outdoor Lighting System
  • Lighting layout design
  • Illumination calculation
  • Cable routing
Indoor Small Power System
  • Load calculation
  • Distribution system design
  • DB and protection selection

Engineering Deliverables

  • Design calculations
  • Detailed drawings (AutoCAD)
  • Bill of Quantities (BOQ)
  • Technical specifications
  • As-built drawings

Standards Followed

  • IEEE Standards
  • IEC Standards
  • BNBC
  • DESCO Engineering Requirements

Conclusion

This project demonstrates EDC Firm’s capability in delivering high-quality electrical design solutions for grid and distribution substations under EPC frameworks, ensuring safety, compliance, and cost-effective engineering.
