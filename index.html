Electrical Design and Consultancy (EDC)

We offer consultation services, power system analysis and design, sizing calculations, and technical reviews. Our expertise covers all voltage systems, generators, transformers, protection and grounding systems, emergency and backup power systems, and comprehensive project management for power plants, grid substations, and industrial projects. Our team is proficient in international and local standards such as IEC, NEC, IEEE, BS, NFPA, BDS, and guidelines from BPDB, PGCB, DPDC, DESCO, ACCORD, and ALLIANCE. We also develop Excel-based electrical programs adhering to industry codes.

Design Discussion

We begin by gathering site data, performing feasibility studies, and creating Single Line Diagrams (SLDs). Our designs incorporate all necessary calculations (short-circuit, grounding, relay coordination) in compliance with IEEE, IEC, NFPA, and BNBC standards.

Engineering & Development

We finalize technical details—selecting equipment, sizing switchgear, and optimizing layouts for safety and efficiency. Our team addresses lightning protection, cable routing, load analysis, and other critical tasks to ensure robust, future-proof solutions.

Delivery & Commissioning

We manage procurement, oversee installation, and conduct rigorous testing (insulation, contact resistance, thermographic) before final commissioning. This ensures a fully functional, standards-compliant system backed by ongoing support.
