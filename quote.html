Let’s Talk About Your Project

Ready to bring your project to life? Reach out to us for expert electrical design and consultancy services that set the benchmark for excellence.

Our expertise spans across domestic, low, medium, high and extra high voltage systems, generators, transformers, protection systems, grounding systems, emergency and backup power systems, and estimation, coordination, execution, testing & commissioning of different power plant, grid substation & industrial projects. Our team is well-versed with international and local standards such as IEC, NEC, IEEE, BS, NFPA & BDS standards and BPDB, PGCB, DPDC, DESCO, ACCORD & ALLIANCE guidelines. We also have a team of programmers who design useful Excel-based electrical programs as per BDS, BS, NEC, NFPA, IEC & IEEE codes.
